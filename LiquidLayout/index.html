Why use a liquid layout?

Liquid layouts bridge the gap between large resolution desktops and small screened mobile devices. When paired with other responsive design techniques, like Media Queries, a liquid layout allows the designer to tailor a user's viewing experience without needing to maintain multiple device-specific copies of his website.
